Energy & Engineering Department

Er. Dr. Saroj Adhikari

Sr. Energy, Developmental & EIA Specialist

Head of Energy & Engineering Department
Professional Overview

Dr. Saroj Adhikari brings over 25 years of professional experience in energy-sector project development, covering engineering design management, project management, environmental & social studies (including resettlement planning), and applied research.

He has successfully led multidisciplinary teams and coordinated with Government agencies, NGOs/INGOs, development partners, and international stakeholders across numerous high-impact assignments.

Most recently, Dr. Adhikari served at the Joint Secretary level as Senior Energy Advisor to the Government of Nepal for the 1200 MW Budhigandaki National Pride Hydropower Project and large-scale solar energy development (up to 400 MW) in Province-2.

His international experience includes collaboration with leading multilateral and bilateral agencies such as ADB, World Bank (MIGA), IFC, and FMO, as well as globally recognized engineering firms and government institutions across Asia, Australia, Europe, and the Middle East.

Dr. Adhikari holds degrees in Civil & Environmental Engineering from the Philippines and a Ph.D. in Renewable Energy & Hydropower from RMIT University, Australia. He is a recipient of multiple national honors, including the prestigious ‚ÄúNepal Bhusan‚Äù award.

Currently, he serves on several Boards of Directors and Review Committees for national energy initiatives, including strategic hydropower planning, river basin planning, and environmental & social assessments under the Ministry of Energy, Water Resources and Irrigation, Government of Nepal.
