About Us

"Production and Market — The Foundation of Prosperity"

The Federation of National Production and Market Protection (FENAMP) was established with the vision of building a self-reliant national economy rooted in Nepali originality by maximizing and mobilizing the country’s available resources. The federation strives to contribute significantly to creating an export-oriented and prosperous society through the promotion of production, industrialization, and market management across key sectors such as agriculture, education, health, and tourism.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower underprivileged agricultural communities by providing the resources, knowledge, and tools necessary for sustainable growth — ensuring the protection and promotion of national production and markets.

Enhancing the livelihoods of farmers and entrepreneurs.

Strengthening value chains across all provinces.

Positioning Nepal as a competitive global producer.
